üìà Projection Methodology

            
Projections for 2026-2028 are based on:

            
    
                
  • Subscriber Growth: Compound growth rate declining from ~80% (2025) to ~40% (2028) as market matures, consistent with analyst models from Payload Space and Quilty Analytics.
    • 
                
  • Revenue: ARPU (Average Revenue Per User) stabilizing around $1,100-$1,200 annually as international mix increases; enterprise and maritime segments growing faster at premium rates.
    • 
                
  • Profitability: Operating margins expanding from ~5% (2024) to ~20% by 2028 as constellation costs amortize and subscriber base scales.
    • 
                
  • Internet Speed: V3 satellites (2026) expected to deliver 300+ Mbps median speeds; ongoing network densification reducing latency to sub-20ms.
    • 
            

            
Note: SpaceX is privately held. Financial figures are estimates from industry analysts. Actual results may vary significantly based on competition, regulation, and technological developments.
